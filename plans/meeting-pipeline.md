# Marketing Stack Meeting Pipeline

**Goal**: Connect with 25 people across the marketing stack to understand how they perceive marketing and what problems they face.

**Split**: 10-15 USA/Canada, 10 Europe

## Target Segments (5 people each)

To maximize span, target these 5 distinct layers of the marketing stack:

### 1. Demand Gen / Paid Acquisition
People who run ads, manage budgets, optimize campaigns.
- Titles: Performance Marketing Manager, Growth Lead, Paid Media Manager, Head of Acquisition
- Companies: DTC brands, agencies, SaaS companies

### 2. Marketing Ops / RevOps
People who wire together tools, manage data flows, attribution.
- Titles: Marketing Operations Manager, RevOps Lead, Marketing Technologist, Marketing Automation Manager
- Companies: Mid-market SaaS, B2B companies

### 3. Content / Brand / SEO
People who create, distribute, and measure organic content.
- Titles: Content Marketing Manager, Head of Brand, SEO Lead, Content Strategist
- Companies: Media companies, marketplaces, SaaS

### 4. Analytics / Data / Attribution
People who measure what works and report on it.
- Titles: Marketing Analyst, Data Lead (Marketing), Attribution Manager, BI Analyst (Marketing)
- Companies: E-commerce, adtech, agencies

### 5. Martech Vendors / Agency Operators
People who build or sell marketing tools, or run agencies.
- Titles: Founder, Product Manager, Solutions Engineer, Agency Director
- Companies: Martech startups, digital agencies, marketing platforms

## LinkedIn Search Queries

Use these in LinkedIn search (People tab), filter by location and company.

| Segment | Search query | Location filter |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Demand Gen | `"performance marketing" OR "paid media" OR "growth marketing"` | USA, Canada, then EU countries |
| Marketing Ops | `"marketing operations" OR "revops" OR "marketing automation"` | Same |
| Content/SEO | `"content marketing" OR "SEO" OR "content strategy"` | Same |
| Analytics | `"marketing analytics" OR "marketing data" OR "attribution"` | Same |
| Vendor/Agency | `"martech" OR "marketing technology" OR "agency founder"` | Same |

**Pro tips:**
- Filter by "2nd degree" connections — easier to get intros
- Filter by company size to get variety (startup, mid-market, enterprise)
- Avoid people with 10k+ followers — they rarely accept cold connects
- Look for people who post or comment actively — they're more likely to engage

## Connection Request Template

Keep it short (under 300 chars). No pitch, no ask for a meeting yet.

```
Hi [Name] — I'm researching how marketing teams actually work
day-to-day across the stack. Your work at [Company] caught my eye.
Would love to connect and exchange perspectives.
```

Variant for vendors/agency:
```
Hi [Name] — I'm exploring how different parts of the marketing
stack connect (and where they break). Your experience at [Company]
seems highly relevant. Happy to connect.
```

## Meeting Ask Message

Send this after they accept your connection (ideally after engaging with 1-2 of their posts first):

```
Hi [Name], thanks for connecting! I'm exploring how marketing teams work across the stack — what tools they use, where things break, what's frustrating. I'd love to hear your perspective as someone doing this hands-on at [Company]. Would you be open to a 30-minute call sometime in the next couple of weeks? Happy to work around your schedule.
```

## Sequence (Timeline)

1. **Days 1-3**: Identify all 25 people, fill the tracker
2. **Days 3-5**: Send connection requests (stagger 5-8/day to avoid LinkedIn limits)
3. **Days 7-14**: Once accepted, engage with 1-2 of their posts before asking for a meeting
4. **Day 10-14**: Send meeting ask (separate template needed — we'll draft when you're ready)
5. **Day 14-21**: Conduct meetings, log findings
