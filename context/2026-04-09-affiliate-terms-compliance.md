# Base44 Affiliate Program — Compliance Rules

Source: `context/base44-affiliate-terms.pdf` (full 17-page agreement)

## Key Rules for Our Sites & Marketing

### Required Disclosures (Section 4.8)
- Every affiliate site MUST include a **prominent, easily accessible disclosure** that we participate in affiliation programs and may receive monetary compensation for referrals.
- Must comply with FTC Endorsement Guides and all applicable laws.

### Tracking & Links (Sections 4.1–4.2)
- All links to Base44 must use the provided **Tracking Codes** — never alter or modify them.
- Tracking codes may only be used for the Program; any other use is a breach.
- Cookies expire after **30 days**; users without cookies won't count as referrals.

### Brand / Trademark Restrictions (Sections 3.7, 4.3–4.4, 8.1–8.2)
- **Never** purchase or register PPC keywords, domains, or email addresses using Base44 trademarks or confusing variations.
- Must add Base44 trademarks as **negative keywords** in any paid search campaigns.
- May only use Base44 Marks as contained in the Ads they provide — no modifications without written approval.
- Never use Ads or Marks in a disparaging or negative manner.
- Never register domains that include Base44 marks or variations.

### Prohibited Marketing Practices (Sections 8.3–8.9)
- **No promoting** Base44's Special Sale Terms.
- **No incentivizing** traffic or conversions (rebates, cashbacks, paid-to-click, discounts) unless approved in writing.
- **No filling forms** or registering users on behalf of referred users.
- **No pop-ups/pop-unders** with Designated Links.
- **No cookie stuffing** (e.g., iframing).
- **No misleading claims** about Base44 or the relationship with Base44.
- Never claim Base44 endorses, supports, or sponsors our site.
- **No client-side software** (toolbars, extensions) using Ads or Designated Links.

### Email / Anti-SPAM (Sections 6.1–6.10)
- **Zero tolerance** for SPAM — only send to people who explicitly opted in.
- Must comply with CAN-SPAM, COPPA, and EU regulations.
- Every email must have valid From/Subject, functioning opt-out link (active 30+ days), and honor opt-outs within 10 days.
- No email to anyone under 18.
- Tracking Codes may be used in emails to registered users of our site.

### Content Restrictions (Section 3.3)
Sites/activities must NOT contain content that:
- Is illegal, offensive, defamatory, obscene, harassing, or discriminatory
- Promotes violence, gambling, or illegal activities
- Infringes third-party IP
- Is directed at children under 13

### Minimum Performance (Section 5)
- Must generate at least **$300 USD/month** in commissions ("Monthly Threshold").
- Below threshold for 3 consecutive months → can be terminated, and accumulated fees voided.
- Terminated affiliates can't rejoin for 6 months.

### Fee Structure (Section 9)
- **First-click attribution** — only the first affiliate to send a user earns the fee.
- Fee is one-time only, for the **first Qualified Purchase** — no renewals, trials, or second purchases.
- Referred user must convert to Premium within **30 days** of referral.
- Packages cancelled within 14 days, refunded, or unpaid don't count.

### Payments (Section 10)
- Monthly reports via Dashboard within 3 weeks of month end.
- Payment within ~45 days after month end, against a valid invoice submitted 30+ days prior.
- Paid in USD only.
- Suspicious activity → up to 60-day payment hold.

### Sub-Affiliates (Sections 11.6–11.11)
- We may use sub-affiliates but must inform Base44 immediately.
- We're fully liable for sub-affiliate compliance.
- Base44 can require us to stop using any sub-affiliate at any time.

### Confidentiality (Section 13)
- Any program-specific terms, pricing, trade secrets etc. are confidential.
- Keep confidential during and after the agreement.

### Termination (Section 12)
- Either party can terminate with 10 days written notice.
- Material breach → immediate termination, no unpaid fees.
- On termination: remove all Tracking Codes, Ads, links, and Base44 Marks promptly.

### Legal (Sections 19.5, 17)
- Governed by **Israeli law**; disputes in Tel Aviv courts.
- Liability capped at 12 months of fees paid.
