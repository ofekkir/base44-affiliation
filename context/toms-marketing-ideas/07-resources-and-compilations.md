## The 13 Viral Marketing Laws (Aug 21, 2025) -- FREE
[Original](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/the-13-viral-marketing-laws)

1. Thompson's Law -- viral spreads via huge accounts, not organic
2. Purple Cow Law -- differentiation drives sharing (Liquid Death $1.4B)
3. Golden Hour Law -- algorithms judge in first 60 min
4. Anti-Polish Principle -- authentic > polished
5. Law of Shitty Clickthroughs -- every tactic decays (Andrew Chen)
6. Gladwell's Law -- Connectors, Mavens, Salespeople
7. Me-First Law -- "now you can" > "we help you"
8. Expertise Curse -- use "the mom test"
9. Matthew's Advantage -- looking successful attracts success
10. 90-9-1 Rule -- 1% create, 9% engage, 90% observe
11. Shareability Spectrum -- job titles predict sharing behavior
12. Post-Purchase Rationalization -- higher spend = stronger promotion
13. Reactance Theory -- "Don't click" > "Click here"
