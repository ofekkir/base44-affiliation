## Key frameworks referenced across posts

- **Brute Force Marketing**: do the work for prospects without asking, gift results
- **Pattern Interruption**: "expected format + unexpected audience"
- **The "$0 with a Twist"**: offer something free IF they do one ridiculous thing
- **"Word of Sight"**: design products to be visibly used (passive promotion)
- **Certificate Marketing**: turn customers into trained sales reps
- **Identity Marketing**: add "-er" to convert actions to identities
- **Literal Marketing**: take industry metaphors and make them physical
- **Praise-Led Marketing**: celebrate others to earn amplification
- **13 Viral Marketing Laws**: Thompson's, Purple Cow, Golden Hour, Anti-Polish, etc.
