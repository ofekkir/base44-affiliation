## How to make competitors look like aliens
**Jul 25, 2025** | [Link](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/how-to-make-competitors-look-like) | Free

Reframe competitors as belonging to a different, inferior category. Five templates:

1. **Tool vs Platform** — They're a tool, you're a platform.
2. **Task vs Transformation** — They help with a task, you deliver a transformation.
3. **Past vs Future** — They're legacy, you're next-gen.
4. **Feature vs Outcome** — They sell features, you sell outcomes.
5. **Doing vs Being** — They help you do something, you help you become something.

Example: Superhuman — "Gmail is a Prius, we're a helicopter."

## My weirdest A/B test
**Mar 7, 2025** | [Link](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/my-weirdest-ab-test-blew-everyones) | Paywalled

Place a blurred product dashboard screenshot behind the signup form. Result: 25% more form completions. Three psychological levers at work:

- **Curiosity gap** — blurred image creates desire to see the real thing.
- **Progress illusion** — feels like the product is already "right there."
- **Trust through preview** — shows there's a real product behind the form.

## The 3-Second Rule
**Jan 3, 2025** | [Link](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/the-3-second-rule) | Free

Every landing page / ad must answer "Is this for me?" in 3 seconds. Three ways to do it:

1. State the job title: "Hey Product Managers..."
2. Name the problem: "Tired of losing leads in spreadsheets?"
3. Call out the demographic: "For teams shipping 10+ releases/month"

"Our AI-powered platform..." fails the test. Lead with the audience, not the product.

## The most powerful CTA
**Nov 24, 2023** | [Link](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/the-most-powerful-cta) | Free

Interactive playgrounds beat free trials every time. CTA effectiveness ranking:

1. "Try it yourself" (best) — interactive playground, zero commitment.
2. "See it in action" — demo video or walkthrough.
3. "Start a free trial" (worst) — requires signup, triggers loss aversion.

Leverages Ownership Bias: once someone plays with something, they feel they own it. Even complex SaaS products can build simplified playgrounds.

## Share love stories, not testimonials
**Nov 17, 2023** | [Link](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/share-love-stories-not-testimonials) | Free

Apply the "Hero Test" — a testimonial should be strong enough to work as the hero section of your landing page. If it can't, it's too weak.

Tactics to collect better testimonials:
- Use creative prompts: "Does our platform give you superpowers? What kind?"
- Run quality-focused review contests (reward best story, not just any review).
- Build a yearly "Wall of Love" page aggregating the best ones.
