## AMA: 20 real questions from real marketers (Apr 2, 2026) -- FREE
[Original](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/ask-me-anything-20-of-my-favorite)

Key answers:
- **B2B Social**: celebrate people solving problems, not your product
- **LinkedIn Outreach**: inbound engagement > automated outbound; build content authority first
- **Podcast Sponsorships**: prioritize creators who personally use products
- **Category Creation**: generally compete in existing categories using "X but for Y" framings
- **2026 predictions**: content production easier through AI making authenticity more valuable; employee LinkedIn accounts > brand accounts; handwritten outreach resurfaces; mini-tools replace free trials
- **Trust**: "Trust is like a currency -- earn it first, then spend."

## Books -- 9 worth reading (Dec 11, 2025) -- FREE
[Original](https://www.marketingideas.com/p/books)

1. **This Is Marketing** (Seth Godin) -- smallest viable audience
2. **Smart Brevity** -- lead with main point, short paragraphs
3. **Building a StoryBrand 2.0** -- customer is the hero, 7-step framework
4. **Influence** (Cialdini) -- 6 drivers: reciprocity, commitment, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity
5. **Magic Words** (Berger) -- "-er" converts behaviors to identities
6. **Loved** -- product marketing positioning
7. **Traction** (Weinberg) -- "Bullseye" method: 3 channels, small experiments, double down on winners
8. **Hit Makers** (Thompson) -- viral = major account broadcasts, not organic spread
9. **How to Win Friends** (Carnegie) -- focus on customer achievements

Bonus: Big Magic, Cash Copy, The Choice Factory.
