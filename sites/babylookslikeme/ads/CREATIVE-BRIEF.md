# Creative Brief

## Tone
Playful, warm, family-emotional. Not techy. Not "AI tool review." Closer to a baby shower game ad than a SaaS ad. Mockups on the LP are illustrative — **never** show a "real screenshot" claim in ad copy.

## Forbidden language (affiliate compliance)
- ❌ "Endorsed by Base44," "official Base44 app," "Base44 partner"
- ❌ "Free Base44 credits" or any incentive language
- ❌ Claims of accuracy, science, paternity, or genetics
- ❌ Any image of a real identifiable child without rights

## Required language
- Where copy implies endorsement or promotion → add "#ad" or "Affiliate" disclosure
- Tag final destination as entertainment, not analysis

## Messaging pillars

### Pillar 1 — The Family Debate (highest priority)
**Hook:** "Mom or Dad? Settle it in 30 seconds."
**Angle:** Two parents arguing playfully → reveal score card.
**Best on:** Meta Reels, TikTok.

### Pillar 2 — Baby Shower Game
**Hook:** "The baby shower game everyone's playing this year."
**Angle:** Group of women at a shower passing a phone, voting before the verdict.
**Best on:** Pinterest, Meta Feed.

### Pillar 3 — Build-It-Yourself UGC
**Hook:** "I built an app to score who my baby looks like. It took 5 minutes."
**Angle:** Creator selfie talking head + screen recording of building flow.
**Best on:** TikTok Spark Ads, Instagram Reels.
**This pillar is the only one that hints at the actual Base44 mechanic — use it to filter out users who won't follow through on building an app.**

## Headline / primary text bank

Hooks (use as opening line of any video or as Meta primary text):
- "Every family has the argument."
- "Mom or Dad? The verdict is in."
- "I needed proof the baby looks like me."
- "Grandma demanded receipts."
- "Tell me you can't build this in 5 minutes."
- "The baby shower game nobody saw coming."

CTA buttons (Meta/TikTok):
- "Learn More" (default)
- "Sign Up" (Build-It UGC pillar)

## Refresh cadence
- Pause any creative whose 7-day CTR drops 30% below ad set average.
- Replace at least 20% of active creatives every 2 weeks.
- Never run more than 6 creatives in one ad set — Meta/TikTok algorithms can't allocate.
