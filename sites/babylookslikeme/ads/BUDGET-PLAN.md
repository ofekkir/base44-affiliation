# Budget Plan

## Working assumption
Commission per Qualified Purchase: **$100** (confirmed).

Affiliate threshold: **$300/mo** → only **3 conversions/mo** required.

Max sustainable CPC ≈ **$1.00** at a 1% click→conversion rate. Most paid channels are in-budget.

## Phase 1 — Test (Month 1)
**Total: $600**

| Channel | Spend | Daily | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meta (Reels/Feed) | $350 | $12 | Highest creative-fit + cheapest CPMs |
| TikTok Spark Ads | $250 | $8 | Validates UGC angle before scaling |

Goal: identify 1–2 winning creatives + measure real LP → Base44 → conversion rate.

**Kill criteria after 14 days:**
- Outbound CTR < 1% on every creative → creative problem, pause and rebuild
- LP arrives but outbound click rate < 15% → LP problem, fix before more spend
- CPC > $1.50 sustained → channel problem, pull budget

## Phase 2 — Optimize (Months 2–3)
**Total: $1,200–1,500/mo**

Distribution depends on Phase 1 winner. Default split if both work:

| Channel | Spend | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Meta | $700 | Scale winning ad sets +20%/week max |
| TikTok | $400 | Refresh Spark Ads weekly |
| Pinterest | $150 | New test — baby shower angle |
| Google Search non-brand | $100 | Tiny test on entertainment queries; strict negatives |
| Reserve | $150 | Creative production |

By end of Month 3 we must see ≥$300 in confirmed commissions (just 3 conversions). At $100/commission this is a very low bar — if we can't clear it, the offer isn't working.

## Phase 3 — Scale (Months 4–6)
**Total: $3,000–5,000/mo**

Only if Phase 2 cleared threshold. Apply 70/20/10:

| Tier | % | Allocation |
|---|---|---|
| Proven (70%) | $2,100–3,500 | Winning Meta + TikTok ad sets |
| Scaling (20%) | $600–1,000 | Pinterest + new geos (UK, AU) + Google non-brand |
| Testing (10%) | $300–500 | YouTube Demand Gen, new creative angles |

## Pacing rules
- **Never raise budget on an ad set by more than 20% in 24h** (Meta/TikTok learning phase resets).
- **3x Kill Rule:** if a creative's CPA exceeds 3× the account average for 7 days, pause it.
- **No retargeting spend until commission volume is proven** — first-click attribution makes LP-bounce retargeting near-worthless.
- **Cap testing at 10% of monthly spend** once scaling.

## Hard stop
If cumulative spend hits **$2,000** and total confirmed commissions are **<$300** (3 conversions), stop all paid acquisition and revisit the LP/offer. The economics aren't there.
